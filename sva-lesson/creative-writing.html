💪 Exercise 4: Conversation Confidence Building

Build unstoppable confidence with these conversation techniques:

🔄 Conversation Flow Techniques

🎯 Agreement Responses:

When someone shares an opinion:

  • "That makes perfect sense."
  • "Everyone feels that way sometimes."
  • "Your point sounds very reasonable."
  • "The facts support what you're saying."
❓ Question Techniques:

To keep conversations flowing:

  • "What makes you think that?"
  • "How does your family feel about it?"
  • "Where do you usually go for that?"
  • "When did you first notice this?"
🚪 Conversation Transitions:

To change topics smoothly:

  • "That reminds me of something..."
  • "Speaking of [topic], I wanted to ask..."
  • "Everyone talks about [new topic] these days."
  • "The situation differs when we consider..."
